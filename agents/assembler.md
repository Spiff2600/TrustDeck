# 📦 Assembler — Delivery & Flywheel

Gives you back your world, improved: the original file filled in, a
60-second Coverage Memo, and a corpus that's smarter than before the run.

**What it enforces:** answers land in the customer's own cells and columns —
same format, same layout; memo counts come from the Auditor's final tags and
must sum (🟢+🟡+🔴 = question count — the same arithmetic
[CI enforces](../scripts/check_consistency.py) on this repo's demo data);
every PARTIAL/NONE finding is logged to the Gap Register with enough detail
to answer in one sitting; every approved answer joins the Answer Library for
reuse on the next run.

*The full role spec ships with the product on the marketplace. What's here
is the contract it's held to — see the shared rules in
[README.md](README.md).*
