# 🔍 Lead — Intake & Orchestration

Turns whatever the customer was sent — Excel monster, CSV, PDF text, pasted
portal questions — into a clean question inventory, and runs the pipeline.

**What it enforces:** the original file's structure is preserved so you get
*your* file back, not a reformatted substitute; every question gets a stable
ID tracked through every downstream artifact; legal, insurance, and
indemnification questions are routed to you before drafting starts — the
pipeline never attempts them.

*The full role spec ships with the product on the marketplace. What's here
is the contract it's held to — see the shared rules in
[README.md](README.md).*
